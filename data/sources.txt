# Gaia Core v0.1 - Approved Ecological Data Sources
# Only these types of public datasets are allowed

NASA Earth Observing System (EOS)
- Earthdata.nasa.gov
- Harmonized Landsat and Sentinel-2 (HLS)

GBIF - Global Biodiversity Information Facility
- gbif.org

CMIP6 Climate Model Ensemble
- esgf-node.llnl.gov

ESA Copernicus Programme
- copernicus.eu

NOAA
- coral reef watch, ocean data

IUCN Red List of Threatened Species
- iucnredlist.org

Other allowed:
- SoilGrids (ISRIC)
- Global Forest Watch
- MODIS / VIIRS satellite products

Note: No social, economic, political, or behavioral data.
All training and inference must remain strictly ecological.
